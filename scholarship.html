Scholarship Applications

OpenStreetMap is a diverse community of mappers, volunteers, humanitarians and advocates promoting open data and open source for everyone to use. Many individuals across Asia are doing amazing work and we would ♥ to hear your stories!

  • Mapped your hometown, city, or region from scratch?
  • Organized events to build your local community of mappers?
  • Contributed code, apps, or tools for OpenStreetMap?
  • Used OpenStreetMap data to promote your organization’s mission?

With support from VISIONS Asia, we are looking for 4 scholars to come to Manila and share their stories.

The deadline for submission is September 7, 2016.

Submit your application!

We will review every application so you can join us in Manila. The scholarships cover full or partial cost of flights, travel expenses, and accommodations.
